About me

From Archaeologist to Data Scientist I'm a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Southampton. I started my career with an undergraduate and masters in Archaeology and while writing my dissertation I noted a gap within the research of automated methods for the detection of archaeological sites. Looking beyond the research within archaeology I found that automation can be done for very challenging tasks and so I decided to pursuit a PhD into the most advanced method out there; Deep Learning.
